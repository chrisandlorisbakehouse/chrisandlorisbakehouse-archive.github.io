Westside Community Farmer's Market

You can find us at the Westside Community Farmer's Market on Saturdays. The market begins at 7:00 am and officially ends at 12:30 pm. However, we typically sell out of our scones & cookies early.

The market runs from April 14th to November 3rd this year.
