We are offering 2 pick-up options on Wed, April 8. You may pick up at our baking facility in Poynette or at Garver Feed Mill in Madison which is a drive-thru setup 4-6pm. It’s pre-order only as we are baking to order each week and have your orders pre-packaged and pre-paid before pickup.
